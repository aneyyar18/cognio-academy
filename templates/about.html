About Cognio Academy

Connecting learners and educators to unlock potential, together.

Our Mission

At Cognio Academy, our mission is to make quality education accessible and personalized for everyone. We strive to bridge the gap between knowledgeable tutors and eager learners, fostering a supportive environment where students can thrive academically and build confidence in their abilities.

Our Story

Cognio Academy was born from a simple idea: finding the right tutor shouldn't be a challenge. Our founders, a group of educators and tech enthusiasts based right here in Hayward, California, experienced firsthand the difficulties students faced in connecting with qualified tutors who matched their specific learning style and needs.

Driven by a passion for education and technology, we set out to build a platform that is intuitive, reliable, and effective. We envisioned a space where students could easily find and connect with vetted tutors, and where educators could share their expertise and make a meaningful impact. Launched in 2023, Cognio Academy is committed to continuously improving and expanding our services to empower learning journeys worldwide.

Our Core Values

Empowerment

We believe in empowering both students and tutors to reach their full potential.

Connection

We foster meaningful connections that facilitate effective learning and mentorship.

Integrity

We operate with transparency, honesty, and a commitment to quality education.
