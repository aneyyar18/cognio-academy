Platform Features

Discover the tools and functionalities that make Cognio Academy the ideal platform for online learning.

Advanced Tutor Search & Filtering

Finding the right tutor is crucial. Our powerful search engine allows you to filter tutors by subject, expertise level, availability, ratings, and even specific keywords. Browse detailed profiles including qualifications, experience, teaching style, and student reviews to make an informed choice.

  • Filter by subject, level, availability, price
  • Read verified student reviews and ratings
  • View detailed tutor profiles and qualifications

Secure Messaging & Easy Scheduling

Communicate directly and securely with potential tutors through our integrated messaging system before booking. Once you've found your match, use our intuitive calendar tool to view tutor availability and schedule sessions that fit seamlessly into your busy life. Get automatic reminders for upcoming sessions.

  • Direct and secure in-platform messaging
  • Integrated calendar for easy booking
  • Automated session reminders

Interactive Virtual Classroom

Experience engaging and effective learning in our state-of-the-art virtual classroom. Features include high-quality video/audio chat, an interactive whiteboard for collaborative problem-solving, screen sharing capabilities, and file sharing for easy exchange of notes and assignments.

  • Real-time video and audio communication
  • Collaborative digital whiteboard
  • Screen sharing and file exchange

Progress Tracking & Feedback System

Monitor your learning journey with session summaries and progress notes provided by your tutor. Our platform facilitates constructive feedback, allowing both students and tutors to rate their experience after each session, ensuring quality and continuous improvement.

  • Session summaries and notes
  • Track learning milestones
  • Two-way feedback and rating system

Safe & Secure Payments

Our integrated payment system ensures secure transactions for booking sessions. Pay per session or purchase package deals with confidence. Tutors receive payments reliably through the platform after sessions are completed.

  • Secure payment gateway integration
  • Multiple payment options (credit card, etc.)
  • Reliable payouts for tutors
